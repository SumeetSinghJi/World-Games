a. Multiplayer Usage: Your account information on AgniSamooh.com, including your username, will be used to

to analyze gameplay patterns, improve game mechanics, and develop new features to enhance user experience 
both within the game and on AgniSamooh.com.

d. Personalized Advertising: We may analyze your gameplay data, including game statistics and preferences, 
to provide personalized advertisements for products or services that align with your interests. This may 
include displaying targeted advertisements within the game or on AgniSamooh.com based on your usage patterns
