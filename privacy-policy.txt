1. Information We Collect
a. Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and other necessary information to facilitate your gameplay and account management.
b. Gameplay Data: We collect and store gameplay statistics, including scores, achievements, and other relevant gameplay data associated with your username.

2. How We Use Your Information
a. Multiplayer Usage: Your username will be publicly displayed during multiplayer sessions to identify you to other players.
b. Game Statistics: We may use your game statistics for leaderboards, in-game rankings, or other interactive features within the game.
c. Improvement and Development: Your data may be utilized to analyze gameplay patterns, improve game mechanics, and develop new features to enhance user experience.

3. Information Sharing
a. Public Display: Your username and game statistics may be publicly displayed within the game, such as on leaderboards or achievement lists, visible to other players.
b. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers to support game functionality, analytics, or marketing purposes.

4. Data Security
We take appropriate measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Choices
a. Account Management: You can update or delete your account information by contacting us directly.
b. Opt-Out: You can choose not to publicly display certain information, though this might limit access to specific game features. To do so simply choose not to sign up in game.

6. Parental Controls and Choices
a. Parental Oversight: Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's account information by contacting us directly.
b. Opt-Out: Users can choose not to display certain information, ensuring a safer gaming experience.
7. Children’s Privacy
a. COPPA Compliance: We comply with COPPA and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
b. Parental Consent: Parental consent is required for the collection of personal information from children under 13, with clear instructions provided within the game.

8. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at the AgniSamooh.com team at support@agnisamooh.com
By using our game, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
